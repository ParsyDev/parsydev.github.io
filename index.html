About Us

Parcyval Studios is an indie game studio with the aim to shape our games to the exact state in which players say "that is exactly what I always wanted to play, why did nobody make this earlier?" We don't work by blueprint — every game is shaped to be unique and fit to what we are aiming for, not orienting onto other games just to make the most profit.

All of our games are forged from community feedback and votings for new features on Discord.

We love helping solo devs by giving them more reach and advertising them — nothing brings more joy than seeing good games by small developers succeeding.

For Creators

Join the Team

This isn't a job listing. Parcyval is about passion. We build something together :) Profits are split based on your role and what you bring to the table. No salaries, no shitty corporation, just people who want to make something ACTUALLY special

Being honest with you: profits are small to nonexistent right now. We're early. This is for people who build out of passion, not because it pays. Keep that in mind.

Shared Profit

No paycheck, but a real cut of what we make together. The studio stays mine — but what we build is equally yours.

Unique Work

Not a copy of whatever's trending to make quick cash. We only build things that feel like they needed to exist.

All Skills Welcome
