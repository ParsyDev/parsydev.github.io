ASCENDING EMPIRES
Verticality
you can not only fight on a horizontal plane anymore, but wherever you want! this encourages building blockades to protect planets
Inventory System
every ship, planet and station has an inventory and can carry a certain amount of items/troops/ships. you can dock onto other ships to transfer item or to board and capture them!
Collisions + Physics
ships can collide, and push/ram each other, which brings new strategic depth to the game
Mod Based
you don't have to play only one mod! every player can play their own FACTION mod, which is synchronized in the lobby!
Discord Server
progress showcase, votings for features and playtests. you can support us (with either donations or volunteer help) or chat with the community, and if you are really special, create mods! Click here to join!
