Privacy Policy for TYPE

Last updated: July 15, 2025

Thank you for playing TYPE (“we”, “us”, or “our”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game TYPE (“the Game”).

1. Information We Collect

When you play TYPE, we may collect the following information:

a. Automatically Collected Data:

  • Device information (such as device ID, operating system, and device type)
  • IP address
  • Gameplay data (such as game progress, high scores, and in-game actions)
  • Crash and error reports

b. Third-Party Services:
TYPE uses the following third-party services:

  • Unity Ads: We use Unity Ads to show in-game advertisements. Unity Ads may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad interactions to provide and improve ad services. For details, please see Unity’s Privacy Policy.
  • Discord SDK: If you connect your Discord account through the Game, the Discord SDK may collect data necessary to link your Discord profile and enable social features like inviting friends or sharing your game status. For details, please see Discord’s Privacy Policy.

We do not collect any personal information that directly identifies you, such as your name or email, unless you choose to contact us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve TYPE
  • Display in-game advertisements
  • Enable social features through Discord
  • Analyze usage to fix bugs and ensure a better player experience

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only:

  • With service providers who help us operate the Game (such as Unity Ads and Discord)
  • If required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights and the safety of our players

4. Children’s Privacy

TYPE is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your information, such as the right to access or delete your data. To make a request, please contact us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date above and, if appropriate, providing notice within the Game.
